# Data License Information

This pipeline is designed to work with the following neuroimaging datasets.
**Each dataset requires independent access approval.** The authors do not
distribute raw data. Comply with all applicable data use agreements (DUAs).

---

## ADNI — Alzheimer's Disease Neuroimaging Initiative

- **Website**: https://adni.loni.usc.edu/
- **Access**: Requires account registration and signed Data Use Agreement at LONI IDA.
- **License**: ADNI Data Use Agreement — non-commercial research only.
- **Citation required**: Yes — cite the ADNI study and relevant publications.

---

## AIBL — Australian Imaging, Biomarkers and Lifestyle Study

- **Website**: https://aibl.csiro.au/
- **Access**: Application required via the AIBL study team.
- **License**: AIBL Data Sharing Policy — research use only.

---

## OASIS-3 — Open Access Series of Imaging Studies

- **Website**: https://www.oasis-brains.org/
- **Access**: Account registration required at oasis-brains.org.
- **License**: Creative Commons CC BY 4.0 with data use agreement.
- **Citation**: LaMontagne et al., 2019, medRxiv.

---

## Custom Datasets

If you use a custom dataset, ensure you comply with the relevant IRB approval,
data protection regulations (HIPAA, GDPR), and any data sharing agreements.

---

## Derived Outputs

This pipeline saves only **derived outputs** (preprocessed images, synthesized PET,
evaluation metrics). Raw data is never modified or redistributed.
